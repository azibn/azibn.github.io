I’m Azib, and I am a PhD student at the University of Warwick. My main research is based on exocomet detection methods and developing new methodologies/frameworks to detect these rare and unique objects using machine learning. I like to think of myself as an Astro-Data Scientist, and look to apply these technologies to my field of work. 

Aside from my work in Astronomy, I have an interest in Data Science and Earth Observation. Prior to starting my PhD, I had a brief stint as an EO Data Scientist at the University of Leicester. I have also had experience in other data science roles, including BP and Warwick Business School. 

I am based in London.
